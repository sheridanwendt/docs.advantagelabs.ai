# How to Grant Access to Google Business Profile (GBP)

**Time required:** 2–3 minutes  
**Access level needed:** Manager (or Primary Owner if requested)

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)  
2. Make sure you’re logged into the Google account that owns the business profile.  
3. Select the correct business location.  
4. Click **Settings → People & Access**.  
5. Click **Add**.  
6. Enter `AEO@AdvantageLabs.ai`.  
7. Choose **Manager** access (Owner access only if specifically requested).  
8. Click **Invite**.  

✅ Done! We’ll receive an email to accept the invitation.
